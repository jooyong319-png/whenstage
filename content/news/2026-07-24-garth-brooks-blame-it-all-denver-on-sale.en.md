Garth Brooks has named Denver as the second stop on his Blame It All On My Roots Arena Tour, following the opening run in Indianapolis. He plays back-to-back nights at Ball Arena on September 4 and 5.

General on-sale begins Friday, July 31 at 10am MT via Ticketmaster. Every seat carries a single $155.25 all-in price, with an eight-ticket limit and no presales.
