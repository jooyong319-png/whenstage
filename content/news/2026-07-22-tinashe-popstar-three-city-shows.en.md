Tinashe has announced "Three Nights With a Popstar," a run of one-off shows built around her upcoming eighth album. The trek opens October 18 at Chicago's The Salt Shed, moves to Brooklyn Paramount on October 24, and wraps November 7 at the Hollywood Palladium in Los Angeles.

Presale tickets went live today at 10am local time, with general on-sale following this Friday, July 24 at 10am local. VIP packages add a post-show DJ set. The shows mark the first live performances of material from Popstar.
