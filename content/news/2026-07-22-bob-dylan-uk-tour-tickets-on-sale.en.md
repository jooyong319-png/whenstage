Bob Dylan's UK tour opens to general ticket sales today, July 22, after a brief fan presale earlier this week. The 10-show run kicks off November 25 in Bournemouth, adds Birmingham, Sheffield and Blackpool, and closes with five nights at London's Royal Festival Hall in December.

Phones and cameras will again be locked away under Dylan's usual Yondr pouch policy. With only one UK run booked for the rest of 2026, seats should move fast now that sales are live.
