Grammy winner Trombone Shorty & Orleans Avenue have announced a new run of fall 2026 shows across the South and East Coast, from Chattanooga and Charlotte to Huntington and Port Chester. Singer-songwriter Abraham Alexander joins as special guest on select dates.

The band also shares an October 23 NJPAC bill with Mavis Staples. Presales are underway ahead of the general on-sale Friday, July 24 at 10am local time.
