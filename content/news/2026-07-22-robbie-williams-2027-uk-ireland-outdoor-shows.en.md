Robbie Williams has confirmed his first 2027 dates: a headline set at Lytham Festival in Lancashire on July 3, then a stadium show at Thomond Park in Limerick, Ireland on July 6, the week after Glastonbury wraps.

General tickets go on sale Friday, July 24 at 10am via Ticketmaster UK and Ireland, with an artist presale and Mastercard early access opening Wednesday. The dates follow his BRITPOP World Tour legs through South America, Australia and New Zealand later this year.
