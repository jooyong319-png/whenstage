Ryan Bingham and The Texas Gentlemen are stretching their Still Gettin' Away With It Tour into mid-November, with newly confirmed stops including Nashville's Ryman Auditorium, Chicago's Riviera Theatre and St. Louis' The Pageant.

The fall run follows the spring release of the pair's album They Call Us The Lucky Ones. After a Wednesday presale, general tickets go on sale Friday, July 24 at 10am local time.
