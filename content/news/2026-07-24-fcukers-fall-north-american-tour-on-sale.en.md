Fresh off supporting Harry Styles in Brazil, New York duo Fcukers have announced a North American headlining run this fall, hitting cities across September and October with support from Baby Wave, SEES00000 and Total Wife on select dates.

The tour follows their acclaimed debut album Ö and a Spotify Radar US Artist nod. Presales start July 28, with general tickets on sale Wednesday, July 29 at 10am local time.
